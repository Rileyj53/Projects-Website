About Us

This website is all about coffee and not just the sugar filled stuff you get from the store down the street either. This website will tell you about the history of coffee, where it originated and how it has developed and evolved over the years. I decided to make this website because ever since I was well, too young I’ve had a fascination with coffee. From roasting my own to now having worked for multiple coffee companies I have experienced almost every part of the coffee making process. A huge part of this website is the Around the World section. This section highlights the history of coffee in different countries along with some recipes you can try at home. Speaking of recipes, the recipe section has a selection of unique coffee recipes anyone can enjoy, whether you have a Keurig or a fancy espresso machine there’s recipes for you! The discover section is where the fun really begins. In the discover section you will either be transported back in history to learn some fun interesting facts about coffee, or you will be taken to a random recipe. Feel free to explore!
